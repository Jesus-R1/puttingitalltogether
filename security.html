Designing for the User

User experience (UX) and visual design are about creating products that are not only functional but also engaging and intuitive. A good UX design improves customer satisfaction and increases usability.

Key Priorities

  • Accessibility: Design for all users, including those with disabilities.
  • Consistency: Maintain a consistent design language across the product.
  • Responsiveness: Ensure the product performs well on all devices and screen sizes.

Challenges

Balancing aesthetics with functionality, making the design accessible without compromising on visual appeal.
