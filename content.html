Optimizing Content for User Accessibility

Content architecture plays a critical role in helping users navigate and find relevant information quickly. A strong information architecture ensures that users can easily access content, reducing friction and improving the overall user experience.

Key Priorities

  • Clear Navigation: Ensure the system is intuitive and easy to navigate.
  • Metadata and Taxonomy: Organize content in a logical structure that is easy to search and categorize.
  • Consistency: Maintain uniformity in naming, labeling, and organization of content across the system.

Challenges

Balancing user needs with business objectives and organizing large amounts of data in a coherent way.
